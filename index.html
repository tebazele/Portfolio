About Me

I'm a software and UI developer with a passion for creating designs that use data analysis and testing to enhance the user experience. I transitioned to software from a career in search engine optimization, content-creation, and marketing because I saw how powerful it is to be able to design applications from the ground-up to serve the very niche needs of each company.


I've worked for years with small businesses, including my own LLC, CraftRanker -- my partner and I spent several years writing a book and creating digital courses teaching crafters and artists how to turn their hobbies into full-time work and make money doing what they love.


Outside of development, I love ultra-light backpacking through Idaho, singing 4-part women's harmony with various groups in the Treasure Valley, and snuggling my bunnies.
